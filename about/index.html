About Me

Hi!

I’m Matteo. A Physicist by training, I have studied in Italy and France and obtained my PhD at the Ecole Polytechnique in Paris, in the field of theoretical solid-state Physics. I started a promising career in academia but soon realised I needed to do something different for a living.

After searching a bit for the next best thing, I turned my attention to the field of AI and started working as a freelance Data Scientist.

Today, I am helping companies solve their business problems and expand their abilities and knowledge by connecting business, data, and people. I have a passion for bridging the gap between people and technology while solving interesting — possibly hard — problems.

I crave learning and sharing knowledge. I have attended and spoken at numerous conferences and events, among which EuroPython and Berlin Python Pizza

In my spare time I like to help out the Python and tech community. I contribute or have contributed at various levels to several initiatives such as PyBerlin, PyLadies, PyData Berlin, and DjangoGirls Berlin.
